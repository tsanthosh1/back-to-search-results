Back to Search Results

Alt + Shift +

Press the shortcut on any page to navigate back to search results.

Step 1: Checks browser history for search pages on the same domain or common search engines
Step 2: If no history found, looks for search pages in tabs to the left
Smart: Recognizes Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Yahoo, Yandex, and Baidu search pages by detecting search-related query parameters like ?q=, ?p=, ?text=, and more
Note: Works on a best-effort basis by analyzing URL patterns - detection may not be perfect in all cases.
